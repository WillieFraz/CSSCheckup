The Monster Trio

The Strawhat pirates are a young famous pirate group within the group they have the Monster trio consisting of Luffy, Zoro and Sanji the strongest fighters in the crew. Luffy is the strongest in the trio and behind him is Zoro and last is Sanji. Whenever the crew faces any dangerous enemies this trio is the one who usually handles it.

  • Monkey D Luffy:

    • Captian of the Strawhat pirates, contains the devil fruit Gomu Nomu wants to become King of Pirates.

  • Roronoa Zoro

    • First mate of the strawhat pirates and their swordsman. Zoro uses three swords to fight, his goal to become the best swordsman of them all.

  • Vinsmoke Sanji

    • The cook of the Strawhat crew and the third strongest in the crew. He uses his feet to fight and calls it "Black Leg Style". Sanji's goal is to find the all blue where all the oceans meet.
